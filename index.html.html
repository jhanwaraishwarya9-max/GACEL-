Key Interpretations
Revenue
Explosive Growth in FY25
Revenue surged 61.3% YoY to ₹75,299L in FY25 — the strongest single-year jump in 5 years, reflecting significant MRO order wins and capacity ramp-up.
Strong upward trend
Profitability
Margin Expansion Story
EBITDA margins improved from 19.9% in FY21 to 36.4% in FY25. Net profit margin expanded from 1.7% to 25.4% — a remarkable 15x improvement in earnings quality.
Excellent efficiency gains
Leverage
Deleveraging in Progress
Debt-to-Equity collapsed from 145x (FY21) to 1.0x (FY25). Total debt declined from ₹33,339L to ₹27,898L while equity grew 120x — a structural transformation.
Rapidly improving
Liquidity
Liquidity Strengthened
Current ratio improved to 2.08x in FY25 (from 1.25x). Quick ratio reached 1.70x — both comfortably above 1x, signaling strong short-term obligations coverage.
Comfortable position
